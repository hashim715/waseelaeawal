Welcome to Waseela E Awal

Waseela e Awal is a platform that encourages the thought that an act of goodwill, regardless of how small, is still an effort, and the most minor of these will in time get together to bring about significance to a cause. Therefore help, in any of it’s forms, from donating funds to acting as a volunteer to a drive of Waseela e Awal, every gesture of charity is valued.
