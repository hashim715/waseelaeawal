Welcome to Waseela E Awal

There is one with soul desiring mend and so he pleads,
but there is also another in greater distress and with needs.
Then it is the composure of both that is assessed,
and for the two He plans, keeping with what He witnessed.
So Together We Stand, To Please Him Through Service,
and express gratitude by being all’s source of bliss.
The name Waseela e Awal represents the idea of one being the source of help and happiness to another through the Almighty,
as such being Zariya e Khuda. Encouraging the thought that an act of goodwill regardless of how small, is still an effort,
and the most minor of these will in time get together to bring about significance to a cause.
